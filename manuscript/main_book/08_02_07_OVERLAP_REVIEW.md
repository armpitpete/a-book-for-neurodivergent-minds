# Chapter 8, Chapter 2, and Chapter 7 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 8 against:

- Chapter 2 — Why Life May Have Felt Harder Than It Looked
- Chapter 7 — Burnout

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 8 has its own job as the masking chapter.

## Chapter 2 job

Chapter 2 explains hidden effort broadly.

Its job is:

- explain why visible performance can hide cost
- give the reader the question: what does it cost me?
- reduce shame around being misread

Chapter 2 should stay broad.

## Chapter 8 job

Chapter 8 focuses on one specific hidden effort: masking.

Its job is:

- explain what masking is
- explain why people mask
- show what masking can cost
- keep safety central
- give small, safer ways to reduce masking

Chapter 8 should not become a general hidden-effort chapter.

## Chapter 7 job

Chapter 7 explains burnout as long-term depletion.

Its job is:

- explain why ordinary rest may not be enough
- focus on reduced demand and protected recovery
- avoid quick-fix recovery promises

Chapter 8 can link masking to burnout, but should not become the burnout chapter.

## Shared language that is allowed

These ideas can appear in more than one chapter:

- hidden effort is real
- looking fine can hide cost
- recovery matters
- shame is not useful data
- the cost of coping counts

This is allowed because each chapter must work on its own.

## Main risk

The main risk is that Chapter 8 may repeat Chapter 2 too much when explaining hidden effort.

Keep the distinction:

- Chapter 2 = hidden effort in general.
- Chapter 8 = masking as one specific hidden effort.
- Chapter 7 = longer-term depletion after repeated strain.

## Editing rule

Chapter 2 = what does it cost me?

Chapter 8 = what am I forcing myself to hide or perform?

Chapter 7 = what keeps draining me faster than I can recover?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 8 is structurally sound as the masking chapter.
