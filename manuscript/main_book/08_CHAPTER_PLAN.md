# Chapter 8 Plan

## Chosen chapter

Chapter 8 — The Cost of Masking

## Place in book

Chapter 8 follows:

- Chapter 7 — Burnout

It should explain one major reason burnout, overload, and recovery debt can build: masking.

## Chapter job

Chapter 8 should explain masking as the effort of hiding, changing, suppressing, copying, or performing to seem acceptable or safe around other people.

It should help the reader understand that masking may protect them in some situations, but it can also have a serious cost.

## Core point

Masking can help a person get through the moment, but it can still cost energy, identity, speech, recovery, and trust in their own signals.

## What Chapter 8 must do

- explain masking in plain language
- avoid blaming the reader for masking
- avoid telling the reader to unmask everywhere
- explain the cost of long-term masking
- connect masking to burnout and hidden effort
- give safer, smaller ways to reduce masking where possible
- stay clear for ND and YP readers
- work as a standalone chapter

## Difference from Chapter 2

Chapter 2 explains hidden effort broadly.

Chapter 8 focuses on one specific hidden effort: masking.

## Difference from Chapter 7

Chapter 7 explains burnout as long-term depletion.

Chapter 8 explains masking as one repeated cost that may help create or worsen depletion.

## Suggested structure

1. What this chapter is for
2. If you can only read one thing
3. Start here if
4. Short version
5. What masking means
6. Why people mask
7. What masking can cost
8. What makes it harder

10. 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes
11. Useful scripts

13. Quick summary

## Avoid

- telling people to stop masking everywhere
- treating masking as fake behaviour only
- ignoring safety
- making the reader feel guilty for surviving
- turning the chapter into identity theory
- repeating all of Chapter 2 or Chapter 7
