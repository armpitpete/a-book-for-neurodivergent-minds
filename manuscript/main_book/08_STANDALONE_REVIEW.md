## Core idea

Masking may help a person get through the moment, but the cost still matters.

The chapter also gives the reader two useful questions:

> What am I forcing myself to hide or perform?

> Is there one safe place where I can reduce that performance a little?

- Treats masking as a survival strategy with a cost.
- Puts the safety warning near the top.
- Gives small, safer ways to reduce masking.

- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
