# Chapter 12, Chapter 2, and Chapter 7 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 12 against:

- Chapter 2 — Why Life May Have Felt Harder Than It Looked
- Chapter 7 — Burnout

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 12 has its own job as the uneven-capacity chapter.

## Chapter 2 job

Chapter 2 explains hidden effort and cost.

Its job is:

- explain why visible performance can hide effort
- explain why looking fine does not prove low cost
- give the reader the question: what does it cost me?

Chapter 2 should stay focused on hidden effort.

## Chapter 12 job

Chapter 12 explains variation.

Its job is:

- explain why a skill may be accessible in one condition but not another
- show why “you did it before” may not prove today
- name conditions that affect capacity
- help the reader adjust the task to current capacity

Chapter 12 should not become a general hidden-effort chapter.

## Chapter 7 job

Chapter 7 explains burnout.

Its job is:

- explain longer-term depletion
- focus on reduced demand and protected recovery
- avoid quick-fix recovery promises

Chapter 12 can mention recovery debt, but should not become the burnout chapter.

## Main distinction

Chapter 2 = what did it cost?

Chapter 12 = what changed between then and now?

Chapter 7 = what keeps draining me faster than I can recover?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 12 is structurally sound as the uneven-capacity chapter.
