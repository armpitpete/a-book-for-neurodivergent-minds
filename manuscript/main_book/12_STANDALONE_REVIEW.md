## Core idea

Being able to do something sometimes does not mean you can do it always.

Capacity can change.

Useful reader question:

> What changed between then and now?

## What works

- Defines uneven capacity plainly.
- Avoids fake/lazy/inconsistent framing.
- Explains why “you did it before” may not prove today.
- Names load, recovery, sensory input, pressure, support, body state, and context.
- Gives practical ways to track conditions, not only results.
- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
