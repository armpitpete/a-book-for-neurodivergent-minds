# Chapter 13, Chapter 8, Chapter 14, and Tool 11 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 13 against:

- Chapter 8 — The Cost of Masking
- Chapter 14 — Rejection Sensitivity and Fear of Getting It Wrong
- Tool 11 — The Misread Map

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 13 has its own job as the social-confusion and after-analysis chapter.

## Chapter 8 job

Chapter 8 explains masking.

Its job is:

- explain hiding or performing to seem acceptable or safe
- show the cost of that performance
- keep safety central

Chapter 13 can mention masking, but should not become the masking chapter.

## Chapter 13 job

Chapter 13 explains social confusion and after-analysis.

Its job is:

- explain why social information can be hard to read
- explain why the mind may replay a social event afterward
- separate useful review from punishment loops
- help the reader separate facts from guesses
- link to Tool 11 when a social moment needs mapping

Chapter 13 should not become only a worksheet or a social-skills lesson.

## Chapter 14 job

Chapter 14 will explain rejection sensitivity and fear of getting it wrong.

Its likely job is:

- explain why possible rejection, criticism, exclusion, or disapproval can feel intense
- explain fear of mistakes and social danger
- reduce shame without dismissing the feeling
- give ways to pause before treating fear as proof

Chapter 13 can prepare for Chapter 14, but should not take over the rejection-sensitivity chapter.

## Tool 11 job

Tool 11 — The Misread Map is the practical worksheet.

Its job is:

- separate intent, action, impact, assumption, and repair
- help the reader map one specific misread moment

Chapter 13 should explain the pattern.

Tool 11 should remain the practical map.

## Main distinction

Chapter 8 = what am I forcing myself to hide or perform?

Chapter 13 = what was unclear, and is this review helping or punishing me?

Chapter 14 = am I treating possible rejection or criticism as proof?

Tool 11 = how do I map this specific misread moment?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 13 is structurally sound as the social-confusion and after-analysis chapter.
