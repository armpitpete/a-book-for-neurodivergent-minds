## Core idea

Body basics are not a reward for coping.

They are part of the floor that makes coping possible.

Useful reader question:

> Has my body floor dropped?

## What works

- Explains body basics plainly.
- Avoids diet advice, weight talk, and moralising food.
- Treats food and body care as capacity support.
- Names food, drink, sleep, medication, pain, toilet needs, temperature, movement, and sensory comfort.
- Makes safe foods and easy foods acceptable.
- Explains why body needs can be hard to notice.
- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
