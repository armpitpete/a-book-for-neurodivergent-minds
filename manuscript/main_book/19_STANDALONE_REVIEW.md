Pass as standalone draft v0.1.

## Core idea

A choice should help you move.

If a choice has become too big to enter, shrink the choice before blaming yourself.

Useful reader questions:

> Is this choice too big?

> What are the real options?

## What works

- Defines decisions and choices plainly.
- Avoids “just choose” framing.
- Avoids moralising decisiveness.
- Explains why too many options can freeze action.
- Separates real choice, fake choice, pressure, and fear.
- Explains safe-enough decisions.
- Gives ways to reduce options and get unstuck.
- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
