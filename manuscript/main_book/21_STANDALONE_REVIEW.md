## Core idea

Good communication should not depend on guessing hidden meanings.

Useful reader questions:

> Was the meaning clear enough?

> What words would make this easier to understand?

## What works

- Defines communication without guesswork plainly.
- Avoids blaming the reader for missing hidden meanings.
- Explains guesswork as a capacity cost.
- Separates clear words, clear expectations, and clear repair.
- Explains that direct does not mean rude.
- Gives practical clarity requests.
- Includes examples for home, school, work, appointments, admin, and relationships.
- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
