# Chapter 24 Plan

## Chosen chapter

Chapter 24 — Friendship, Love, and Being Misread

## Place in book

The current working table of contents places Chapter 24 in Part 3 — What Helps in Real Life.

It follows:

- Chapter 23 — Repair, Apology, and Not Carrying Everything

It comes before:

- Chapter 25 — Work, School, and Being Treated Fairly

## Chapter job

Chapter 24 should explain friendship, love, care, attraction, attachment, and being misread without shame.

It should help the reader understand that relationships can be confusing when signals are hidden, social rules are unclear, feelings are intense, or other people misread neurodivergent communication.

It should also show that being misread does not mean the reader's feelings are wrong, dangerous, childish, or fake.

## Core point

Being misread does not make your care wrong.

Relationships need clearer words, safer pacing, and respect for both people.

## What Chapter 24 must do

- explain friendship, love, and being misread in plain language
- avoid shaming intense feelings, loyalty, attachment, attraction, or confusion
- separate care, friendship, attraction, love, attachment, recognition, comfort, and safety
- explain why relationships can feel unclear or overwhelming
- explain how masking, social confusion, rejection sensitivity, and guesswork can affect relationships
- show ways to slow down, check meaning, and protect both people
- include examples for friendship, crushes, romantic interest, family, online connection, and being misunderstood
- connect relationships to communication, repair, boundaries, rejection sensitivity, social confusion, and self-kindness without retelling those chapters
- stay clear for ND and YP readers
- work as a standalone chapter

## Difference from Chapter 23

Chapter 23 explains repair, apology, and responsibility.

Chapter 24 explains relational meaning, misreading, care, attachment, friendship, and love.

The two overlap when a relationship needs repair, but Chapter 24 should not become another apology chapter.

## Difference from Chapter 25

Chapter 25 will explain work, school, and being treated fairly.

Chapter 24 can mention school/work relationships, but should focus on personal connection and being misread.

## Avoid

- shaming intense feelings
- calling attachment childish
- making love sound simple
- implying every strong feeling should be acted on
- turning the chapter into dating advice only
- taking over the repair chapter
- taking over the work/school fairness chapter
- too many scripts
