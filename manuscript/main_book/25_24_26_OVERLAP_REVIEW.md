# Chapter 25, Chapter 24, and Chapter 26 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 25 against:

- Chapter 24 — Friendship, Love, and Being Misread
- Chapter 26 — Systems, Forms, and Official Language

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 25 has its own job as the work, school, and fairness chapter.

## Chapter 24 job

Chapter 24 explains friendship, love, care, attraction, attachment, and being misread.

Its job is:

- explain relational misreading without shame
- reduce panic around friendship and love confusion
- separate care, attraction, attachment, recognition, comfort, and safety
- show how relationships can be made clearer and safer through pacing, consent, and direct words

Chapter 25 can mention relationships in work or school, but should not become the friendship, love, or being-misread chapter.

## Chapter 25 job

Chapter 25 explains work, school, and being treated fairly.

Its job is:

- explain fairness, access needs, and practical support in structured settings
- separate personal failure from bad fit, unclear demand, missing support, and unfair treatment
- show support as barrier reduction rather than special favour or task avoidance
- help the reader ask for practical adjustments that make the real task possible

Chapter 25 should not become the systems, forms, and official-language chapter.

## Chapter 26 job

Chapter 26 will explain systems, forms, and official language.

Its likely job is:

- explain why forms and official systems can become inaccessible
- reduce shame around paperwork, portals, bureaucracy, and official wording
- show how to translate official language into real tasks
- help the reader find the next required action without being overwhelmed by the whole system

Chapter 25 can mention forms and official processes, but should stay focused on work/school fairness and support.

## Main distinction

Chapter 24 = how can friendship, love, and being misread be understood without shame?

Chapter 25 = what support or fairness is needed in work or school?

Chapter 26 = what does this system, form, or official wording actually require?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 25 is structurally sound as the work, school, and being-treated-fairly chapter.
