# Chapter 26, Chapter 25, and Chapter 27 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 26 against:

- Chapter 25 — Work, School, and Being Treated Fairly
- Chapter 27 — Money, Admin, and Avoiding Shame Spirals

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 26 has its own job as the systems, forms, and official-language chapter.

## Chapter 25 job

Chapter 25 explains work, school, and being treated fairly.

Its job is:

- explain fairness, access needs, and practical support in structured settings
- separate personal failure from bad fit, unclear demand, missing support, and unfair treatment
- show support as barrier reduction rather than special favour or task avoidance
- help the reader ask for practical adjustments that make the real task possible

Chapter 26 can mention work/school forms, but should not become the work/school fairness chapter.

## Chapter 26 job

Chapter 26 explains systems, forms, portals, paperwork, official wording, and bureaucracy.

Its job is:

- explain why forms and official systems can become inaccessible
- reduce shame around paperwork, portals, bureaucracy, and official wording
- show how to translate official language into real tasks
- help the reader find deadline, required action, evidence needed, contact route, and consequence
- help the reader find the next required action without being overwhelmed by the whole system

Chapter 26 should not become the money/admin shame-spiral chapter.

## Chapter 27 job

Chapter 27 will explain money, admin, and avoiding shame spirals.

Its likely job is:

- explain why money/admin tasks can trigger shame and avoidance
- separate current practical action from self-blame
- help the reader stabilise one admin or money task at a time
- reduce spirals around debt, bills, missed payments, budgeting, and delayed action

Chapter 26 can mention money and admin systems, but should stay focused on decoding official wording and systems.

## Main distinction

Chapter 25 = what support or fairness is needed in work or school?

Chapter 26 = what does this system, form, or official wording actually require?

Chapter 27 = what money/admin action is needed without entering a shame spiral?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 26 is structurally sound as the systems, forms, and official-language chapter.
