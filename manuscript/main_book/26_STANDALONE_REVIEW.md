## Core idea

Official language often hides the real task.

The first job is to translate the system into one clear next action.

> What does this system actually want me to do next?

- Gives examples for forms, letters, portals, emails, policies, appointments, and official instructions.
- Includes 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Includes practical scripts.
- Ends with three quick-summary bullets.
