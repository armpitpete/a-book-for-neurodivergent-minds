# Chapter 27, Chapter 26, and Chapter 28 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 27 against:

- Chapter 26 — Systems, Forms, and Official Language
- Chapter 28 — Health, Appointments, and Being Believed

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 27 has its own job as the money, admin, and shame-spiral chapter.

## Chapter 26 job

Chapter 26 explains systems, forms, portals, paperwork, official wording, and bureaucracy.

Its job is:

- explain why forms and official systems can become inaccessible
- reduce shame around paperwork, portals, bureaucracy, and official wording
- show how to translate official language into real tasks
- help the reader find deadline, required action, evidence needed, contact route, and consequence
- help the reader find the next required action without being overwhelmed by the whole system

Chapter 27 can mention systems and forms, but should not become the systems/forms decoding chapter.

## Chapter 27 job

Chapter 27 explains money, admin, and avoiding shame spirals.

Its job is:

- explain why money/admin tasks can trigger shame and avoidance
- separate practical problem, risk, next action, support, and self-blame
- help the reader stabilise one admin or money task at a time
- reduce spirals around debt, bills, missed payments, budgeting, unopened letters, and delayed action

Chapter 27 should not become the health, appointments, and being-believed chapter.

## Chapter 28 job

Chapter 28 will explain health, appointments, and being believed.

Its likely job is:

- explain why health needs can be hard to describe, prove, or get believed
- help the reader prepare for appointments and communicate symptoms or needs clearly
- separate being calm from being fine
- reduce shame around needing care, follow-up, support, or advocacy

Chapter 27 can mention appointments or bills, but should stay focused on money/admin and shame spirals.

## Main distinction

Chapter 26 = what does this system, form, or official wording actually require?

Chapter 27 = what money/admin action is needed without entering a shame spiral?

Chapter 28 = what health need, appointment issue, or belief barrier needs support?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 27 is structurally sound as the money, admin, and shame-spiral chapter.
