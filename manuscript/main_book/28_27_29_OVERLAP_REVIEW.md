# Chapter 28, Chapter 27, and Chapter 29 Overlap Review

## Scope

Reviewed Chapter 28 against:

- Chapter 27 — Money, Admin, and Avoiding Shame Spirals
- Chapter 29 — Rest, Recovery, and Not Earning Exhaustion

## Verdict

The overlap is acceptable.

No immediate rewrite is needed.

Chapter 28 has its own job as the health, appointments, and being-believed chapter.

## Chapter 27 job

Chapter 27 explains money, admin, and avoiding shame spirals.

Its job is:

- explain why money/admin tasks can trigger shame and avoidance
- separate practical problem, risk, next action, support, and self-blame
- help the reader stabilise one admin or money task at a time
- reduce spirals around debt, bills, missed payments, budgeting, unopened letters, and delayed action

Chapter 28 can mention health admin or appointment systems, but should not become the money/admin chapter.

## Chapter 28 job

Chapter 28 explains health, appointments, and being believed.

Its job is:

- explain why health needs can be hard to describe, prove, or get believed
- help the reader prepare for appointments and communicate symptoms, impact, support needs, and follow-up clearly
- separate being calm from being fine
- reduce shame around needing care, follow-up, written information, support, or advocacy

Chapter 28 should not become the rest, recovery, and exhaustion chapter.

## Chapter 29 job

Chapter 29 will explain rest, recovery, and not earning exhaustion.

Its likely job is:

- explain rest as a need, not a reward earned by collapse
- separate recovery from laziness or failure
- help the reader notice capacity loss before exhaustion becomes severe
- support planned recovery after health, sensory, emotional, social, work, or admin load

Chapter 28 can mention recovery, but should stay focused on health communication, appointments, belief barriers, and follow-up.

## Main distinction

Chapter 27 = what money/admin action is needed without entering a shame spiral?

Chapter 28 = what health need, appointment issue, or belief barrier needs support?

Chapter 29 = what rest or recovery is needed before exhaustion becomes damage?

## Current status

No immediate rewrite needed.

Chapter 28 is structurally sound as the health, appointments, and being-believed chapter.
