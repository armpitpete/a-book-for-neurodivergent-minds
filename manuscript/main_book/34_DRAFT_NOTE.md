# Chapter 34 Draft Note

## Draft completed

Chapter 34 has a first working draft.

## Chosen title

Keeping Things Going Without Starting Over

## Why this title was chosen

No Chapter 34 title or brief existed in the repo.

Chapter 33 covered getting help without giving up control.

Chapter 34 follows naturally by helping the reader keep useful supports, systems, and changes alive over time, including after gaps.

## Main job

Chapter 34 explains how to return to useful things without treating every slip as a full failure or a full restart.

## Main choices

- Framed useful things as allowed to slip without becoming worthless.
- Introduced return points.
- Separated maintenance, adjustment, and replacement.
- Explained when a system does not fit.
- Added keeping the useful part, making the restart smaller, building for bad days, leaving breadcrumbs, and avoiding full rebuilds.
- Added 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes actions.
- Added practical scripts.
- Linked back to Chapters 29, 30, 31, 32, and 33.
- Ended with three quick-summary bullets.

## Boundary notes

Chapter 33 is the support-without-being-taken-over chapter.

Chapter 34 is the return-points and maintenance chapter.

It should not become the final book conclusion, a full life philosophy chapter, a productivity system, or a crisis plan.
