## Why this title was chosen

## Main job

Chapter 35 explains how to use the book safely, selectively, and without turning it into another pressure system.

## Main choices

- Framed the book as a support shelf, not a test.
- Made clear the reader does not need to read everything in order.
- Made clear the reader can take one sentence, script, action, chapter, or tool.
- Explained choosing a chapter by need.
- Explained using the 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes structure without pressure.
- Explained using scripts without apology.
- Explained leaving what does not fit.
- Explained returning later without shame.
- Introduced the supplement as practical support, not homework.
- Added a small closing note: the reader is not a problem to solve.
- Added practical scripts.
- Ended with three quick-summary bullets.

## Boundary notes
