# Standalone Chapter Check

Each chapter must work as a separate reading unit.

A reader should be able to open one chapter and get useful help without reading the whole book first.

## Required checks

Each chapter should have:

1. A clear opening point
2. A short version or quick-start section
3. Plain explanation
4. Common signs or examples
5. What makes it harder
6. What helps
7. Small actions for now
8. Useful scripts or phrases
9. A short recap

## Editing rule

When revising a chapter, do not assume the reader remembers previous chapters.

Repeat only the small amount of context needed for that chapter to work.

Do not overload the reader with cross-references.
