# Supplement ND and YP Review v0.1

## Scope

Reviewed supplement modules 7 to 12 for neurodivergent readers and young people.

Reviewed modules:

- 07 — One-Thing Ticket
- 08 — Noise Budget
- 09 — Truth Without Explaining
- 10 — Aftercare Loop
- 11 — The Misread Map
- 12 — The Quiet Handover

## Review rule

Could a tired neurodivergent reader or young person use this without having to decode it?

## Verdict

Pass for first expanded draft.

The modules are short, practical, literal, and usable.

They are not final proof text yet, but they are good enough to stay as working drafts.

## What works

- Each module has a clear use case.
- Each module has a default line.
- Each module uses short sections.
- Each module gives 30 seconds / 3 minutes / 30 minutes help.
- The scripts are direct and reusable.
- The language avoids clever or cryptic framing.
