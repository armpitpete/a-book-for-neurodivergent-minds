# Aftercare Loop

## Status

Expanded draft v0.1.

## Use this when

Use this after overload, shutdown, meltdown, conflict, a hard appointment, a bad social moment, or a high-pressure day.

Use it before you analyse what happened.

## Default line

“Aftercare first. Analysis later.”

## What this tool does

Aftercare Loop helps you recover before you judge yourself.

Many people try to explain, apologise, fix, or understand everything while their system is still raw.

That often makes the crash longer.

Aftercare comes first because a tired system cannot think clearly.

## 30 seconds

Choose one body support:

- water
- food
- quiet
- darkness
- blanket
- fresh air
- no talking
- safe person nearby

Do that before analysis.

## 3 minutes

Use this loop:

1. Body first.
2. Less input.
3. One safe next step.
4. No self-trial yet.

Example:

- drink water
- sit in quiet
- send “I need time”
- no replaying the whole event yet

## 30 minutes

Make a recovery pocket.

Ask:

1. What does my body need?
2. What input can stop?
3. What message must be sent, if any?
4. What can wait until tomorrow?

Example:

- body: food and sleep
- input: no more messages
- message: “I am not able to talk tonight”
- wait: explaining what happened

## Useful scripts

“I need aftercare before I can talk about this.”

“I am not ready to analyse it yet.”

“I will come back to this when I am steadier.”

## Quick summary

- Recover before you analyse.
- Meet body needs first.
- Do not turn the aftermath into a self-trial.
